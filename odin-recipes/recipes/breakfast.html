Breakfast

Description

This breakfast will make you feel awesome. No better way to start your day.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Oatmeal
  • Olive Oil
  • Turkey Bacon
  • Chocolate Chips
  • Hot Sauce

Steps

  1. Start heating your nonstick pans.
  2. Crack four eggs into a pan. Add a few drops of water and cover partially with lid.
  3. Place bacon slices in other pan.
  4. Microwave oatmeal until cooked.
  5. Mix in olive oil with oatmeal and top with chocolate chips.
  6. Remove items from pans and season.
  7. Serve and enjoy.
